Why +350 kcal (not +500)?
The research on realistic bulking:
A +500 kcal surplus sounds ideal but for a moderately active teenager/young adult with 2kg dumbbells and bodyweight training, ~60% of that extra energy goes to fat, not muscle. At your stage:

• Muscle synthesis limit = ~0.25–0.4 kg/week at natural level
• +300–400 kcal hits that ceiling perfectly
• +500+ kcal just adds excess fat at this training intensity

Formula: TDEE × 1.55 + 350 kcal = lean bulk
Expected: ~1.5–2 kg lean gain/month if you hit protein and train consistently.
Equipment Strategy
🏋️ 2kg dumbbells: Use for isolation + high-rep volume (20–30 reps, slow 4-sec negatives, pauses). These weights aren't limiting — your time-under-tension and technique are.

💪 Bodyweight: Push-ups, dips, squats, lunges, rows — these build real muscle when done with progressive overload (more reps, tempo, pauses).

🏠 No equipment: Wall sits, planks, bridges, mountain climbers — for endurance and core work that supports all compound movements.
